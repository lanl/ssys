# Code of Conduct

ssys contributors are expected to keep project spaces professional, respectful,
and focused on constructive technical collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of differing experience levels and viewpoints.
- Give and receive technical criticism with specific, actionable context.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its scientific software goals.
- Respect maintainers' decisions about scope, release readiness, and review
  standards.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Sustained disruption of issues, pull requests, or maintainer workflows.
- Publishing private contact information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would make participation unsafe or hostile for others.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer at
hlavacek@lanl.gov. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject
contributions, or block participants when needed to protect the project space.
